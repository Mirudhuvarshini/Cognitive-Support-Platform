## AI Powered Cognitive Support Platform for Neurodivergent Individuals

Navigating a world designed for neurotypical minds can lead to burnout, sensory overload, and task paralysis. **Neural Compass** bridges that gap with:

- Structured task breakdowns powered by AI
- Sensory regulation and self-care tools
- Gamified cognitive training games
- Emergency SOS with live location
- A 24/7 AI companion with live voice conversation
- Caregiver monitoring and community support
